Apple-Honey Cake Recipe

Nothing evokes the cozy essence of fall quite like the aroma of a warm, spiced apple cake. Our Apple-Honey Cake is tender, moist, and filled with chunks of roasted apples, sweetened naturally with honey and brown sugar. Enhanced with a comforting blend of cinnamon, nutmeg, and cardamom, this cake is perfect for holiday gatherings or a quiet afternoon treat.

This recipe is inspired by cherished family traditions and offers a way to bring a bit of history and sweetness to your kitchen.

Why You’ll Love This Apple-Honey Cake

  • Moist and Tender: Roasted apples and honey keep this cake soft and flavorful.
  • Richly Spiced: Cinnamon, nutmeg, and cardamom add warmth and depth.
  • Make-Ahead Friendly: Flavors develop beautifully if baked a day before serving.
  • Versatile: Oil-based and pareve, it suits both dairy and non-dairy pairings.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium Honeycrisp apples, peeled and cubed (¼-inch)
  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¾ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¾ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ¾ teaspoon cardamom
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup honey
  • ½ cup brewed coffee, cooled

Instructions

Step 1: Roast the Apples

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss cubed apples with 1 tablespoon olive oil in a 9×13-inch baking dish. Bake for about 45 minutes, stirring occasionally, until apples are soft and translucent. Stir in grated ginger and set aside to cool slightly.

Step 2: Prepare the Cake Batter

Reduce oven temperature to 325°F (160°C). Line a 10-inch springform pan with parchment paper, ensuring some paper comes up the sides. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cardamom. In another bowl, whisk eggs with the remaining 2 tablespoons oil until combined. Add brown sugar, cider vinegar, and vanilla; whisk until frothy. In a measuring cup, mix honey and cooled coffee until honey dissolves.

Step 3: Combine Ingredients

Alternately fold dry ingredients and honey-coffee mixture into the egg mixture in three batches. Fold in cooled roasted apples gently to avoid overmixing.

Step 4: Bake

Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth the top. Bake for 1 hour 5 minutes to 1 hour 15 minutes, until top is browned and a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let cool for 1 hour before removing the springform sides.

Step 5: Chill and Serve

Refrigerate the cake overnight for the flavors to develop. Remove from the fridge and let it sit at room temperature for at least 1 hour before serving. Pair with non-dairy vanilla ice cream or apple jam for a decadent touch.

Pro Tips for the Perfect Apple-Honey Cake

  • Roasting apples first enhances their natural sweetness and prevents the cake from being too dense.
  • Honey acts as a natural sweetener, reducing the need for white sugar and keeping the cake moist.
  • Baking the cake a day ahead allows the flavors to meld beautifully.
